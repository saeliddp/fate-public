Google or Not?

Test How Well You Know Google!

How to Play:

1. You will be shown two sets of results for each search term. You will have 30 seconds to guess which side is from Google.

2. If you get it right, you will earn 10 points. For being wrong, you lose 5 points

3. If you make no selection after 30 seconds, you will earn 0 points

4. You will then be directed to the next pair of results for a new search term

5. There will be 20 rounds. Good luck!
