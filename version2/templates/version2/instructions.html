How to Play

1. You will have 30 seconds to choose between the two sets of results

2. Select the set of results that came directly from Google

3. Press submit

4. If you choose correctly, you will score 10 points; if you choose the wrong option, you will lose 5 points

5. If you make no selection after 30 seconds, you will score 0 points

6. You will then be directed to the next pair of results for a new search term

7. There will be 20 rounds. Good luck!
