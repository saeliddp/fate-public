Google or Not?

#}

How to Play:

Guess which is from Google!
You will be shown two sets of results for each search term. You will have 30 seconds.
For being right, +10 points.
For being wrong, -5 points.
No answer, 0 point.
There will be 20 rounds. You will get a badge after finished the game.
Good luck!
